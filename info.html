Festival details

All the essentials

Private property near Wisemans Ferry, 90 minutes from Sydney. River mornings, dancing nights, and a full weekend of ritual-level coziness on 1-3 May.

Venue

The party will be held on a private property near Wisemans Ferry. The images above and below give you a quick glimpse of the space. BYO everything. We will put on a dinner on Saturday evening by the open bonfire.

Estimated cost is around $120 per person for the weekend based on 70 people. We will confirm once the poll results are in.

Theme + styling

Samhain marks the shift from summer into winter. Dress in cozy fur, cloaks, or your own take on old pagan festival visuals.
